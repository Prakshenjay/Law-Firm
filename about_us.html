DIRECTOR .

Advocate Aditya Krishna Pandey

   Aditya Krishna Pandey is a litigant who is the Partner at Pandey Law Chambers in the heart of the city of Bangalore at Residency Road. He is a product of School Of Law Christ deemed to be University.

      He is also credited of being the founder of Law and You a start up initiative that he had pioneered in his days of law school. Aditya's regions of practice are diverse.       At the juncture of Leadership in Pandey Law Chambers, he is known for continuously motivating his juniors to strive for the best in any task assigned to them. Advocate Pandey believes in clean and efficient work and he takes it upon himself to make sure that every person in the firm adheres to it.
